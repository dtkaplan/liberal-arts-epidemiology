A trial is likely to be underpowered, biased, or misleading if which of the following occurs:
\begin{MultipleChoice}
\wrong{A large number of participants is lost to follow up.}
\wrong{A substantial number of participants do not adhere to the study intervention.}
\wrong{Baseline variables are not balanced between intervention groups.}
\wrong{All of the above lead to these problems.}
\end{MultipleChoice}
