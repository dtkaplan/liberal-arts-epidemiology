An inherited condition called Fanconi's anaplastic anemia increases a person's risk of leukemia, liver, and skin cancers.  Which of the following is true?
\begin{MultipleChoice}
\wrong{This is a necessary condition.}
\wrong{This is a sufficient condition.}
\wrong{This is both a necessary and sufficient condition.}
\correct{This is neither a necessary nor a sufficient condition.}
\end{MultipleChoice}
